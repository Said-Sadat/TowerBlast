Command Your Army!

When the undead rise and attempt to take over the world it is your duty to go and stop them! Pick up your sword and lead your army to battle against the undead and defeat them before there are too many. Choose from a variety of troops and deploy them to fight against the opponents but make sure you are careful as misplacing them could end up getting them killed early and leave you defenceless.

Play Now!

Enjoy a range of features available in the game

Choose your Strategy

Choose which troops you want to deploy to fight for you! Select a tactic before going into battle to be able to set your formation for Towers that contain archers and can fire from a long distance

Enter The BattleField!

Fill the Rage Gauge by killing opposition troops and join your army in the battlefield by clicking one of your troops and taking over. Be careful though, as when you are in the battlefield you will not be able to deploy more troops so think carefully.
